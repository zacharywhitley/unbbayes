Add Goal

You should begin definig what are the goals of your Probabilistic Ontology.

The Requirements Discipline consist in defining what are the goals of the ontology and what hypothesis will be used to achieve this goals. The objects of the modeling discipline will be created based on this goals, so, you have to try make the most complete list of goals possible.


Samples:
>> GOAL 1: Identify whether a given procurement should be inspected and/or audited.
>> GOAL 2: Identify whether the committee of a given procurement should be changed.
