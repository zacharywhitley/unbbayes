Add Hypothesis

The Hypothesis is the information that will be used to achieving a goal. It should be a query or a evidence: a query is a question which the answer is important to achieve the goal; an evidence is informations used to solve the query.


Samples:

GOAL 1: Identify whether a given procurement should be inspected and/or audited.
QUERY A: Is there any relation between the committee and the enterprises that participated in the procurement?
EVIDENCE 1: Look for member and responsible person of an enterprise who are related (mother, father, brother, or sister);
EVIDENCE 2: Look for member and responsible person of an enterprise who live at the same address.
